    • ABOUT ME

    Welcome to my candle world! I'm a passionate candle maker based in Montclair, New Jersey, with roots in Paterson. At the heart of my craft is the art of hand-poured soy wax candles, each lovingly crafted in upcycled vessels. With every candle I create, you'll find a touch of peace and a whole lot of love. I'm proud to have graduated from Howard University in 2010, and I'm excited to share my candle creations with you. Every candle in my collection is meticulously hand-poured using eco-friendly soy wax and upcycled vessels. It's not just about creating beautiful candles; it's about doing it sustainably. What truly sets my candles apart is the passion and care I infuse into each one. They aren't just products; they're a labor of love. When you light one of my candles, you're embracing a piece of my heart and soul. And let's talk about scent! The fragrances I've carefully curated are not just strong; they're unforgettable. They'll transform your space, creating an ambiance that lingers long after the flame has been extinguished.
